I'm a small batch roaster out of Tamworth, New Hampshire.

I've been in love with coffee my whole life but working in coffee for about 6 years.

It's been a natural progression of working in cafes to working in a more coffee focused cafe to roasting and buying coffee for a major grocery chain.

I go to all the coffee expos, shows, and contest that are in my reach to find out new information and techniques.

I also visit all the coffee shops I can find to see different styles and approaches people take with coffee.

I find coffee to be a culture and way of life.

It can be a ritual one does every morning or an art form.

To me it's both. I like a cup to wake up too, but I appreciate the care a specialty coffee shop puts into every cup. I also treat coffee as a desert after a good meal. Coffee fits so many aspects of life and means something different to everyone.
